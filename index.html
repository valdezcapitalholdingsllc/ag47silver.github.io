Why Silver

Silver is both a monetary metal and a critical industrial resource. It is essential for electronics, solar energy, medical applications, and national defense — while also serving as a hedge against currency debasement and systemic risk.

Critical Mineral Status

Silver (Ag) is increasingly recognized as a critical mineral due to its irreplaceable role in advanced technology, energy infrastructure, and supply-chain security.

AG47 Thesis

AG47 represents the convergence of physical scarcity, monetary history, and industrial necessity. As demand accelerates and supply constraints tighten, silver’s strategic importance continues to grow.
