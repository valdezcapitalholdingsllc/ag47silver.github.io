AG47 Silver is a conviction-driven silver thesis built on one simple belief: **physical silver is structurally undervalued in a manipulated paper market**.

This site exists to document the case for silver as a long-term store of value, a strategic hedge against monetary debasement, and an asset whose real price has yet to be discovered.

Why Silver Matters

Silver is historically undervalued relative to gold while carrying **greater industrial importance and tighter supply dynamics**.

The modern silver market is dominated by paper contracts that vastly exceed available physical supply. As industrial demand rises and trust in fiat systems erodes, the disconnect between paper price and physical value becomes unsustainable.
